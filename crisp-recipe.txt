Crisp

Filling
4 cups fruit
1 cup sugar (peaches 1/2 cup maybe)
1/2 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon cinamon (use more)

Mix in bowl, add 1/4-1/2 cup water
Put in 8x8x2 baking dish
grease dish first

Topping
1 cup flour
3/4 rolled oats
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup melted butter

mix in bowl, put over filling

Bake @ 350 till done (aprox. 1 hour)
